Bài làm:

a) $\dfrac{sin(3x)}{cos(3x) - 1} = 0$
ĐKXĐ $cos(3x) \neq 1$

$3x \neq 2k\pi~\{k \in Z\}$

$x \neq \dfrac{2\pi}{3}~\{k \in Z\}$

$\dfrac{sin(3x)}{cos(3x) - 1} = 0 \iff sin(3x) = 0$

$\iff 3x = 2k\pi \{k \in Z\}$

$\iff x = \dfrac{2\pi}{3}~\{k \in Z\}$ (không thỏa mãn điều kiện)

Vậy phương trình trên vô nghiệm

b) $cos(2x)cot(x - \dfrac{\pi}{4}) = 0$

ĐKXĐ: $sin(x - \dfrac{\pi}{4}) \neq 0$

$\iff x - \dfrac{\pi}{4} \neq k\pi~\{k \in Z\}$

$\iff x \neq k\pi + \dfrac{\pi}{4} \{k \in Z\}$

$cos(2x)cot(x - \dfrac{\pi}{4}) = 0$

$\iff \Bigg[\displaylines{cos(2x) = 0 \\\ cos(x - \dfrac{\pi}{4}) = 0}$

$\iff \Bigg[\displaylines{2x = k\pi + \dfrac{\pi}{2}\{k \in Z\} \\\ x - \dfrac{\pi}{4} = k\pi + \dfrac{\pi}{2} \{k \in Z\}}$

$\iff \Bigg[\displaylines{x = \dfrac{\k\pi}{2} + \dfrac{\pi}{4}\{k \in Z\} \\\ x = k\pi + \dfrac{3\pi}{4} \{k \in Z\}}$
