a) $y = \dfrac{1 + cos(x)}{sin(x)}$

b) $y = \sqrt{\dfrac{1 + cos(x)}{1 - cos(x)}}$

c) $y = tan(x - \dfrac{\pi}{3})$

d) $y = cot(2x + \dfrac{\pi}{6})$

Bài 1:
a)

b)

ĐKXĐ $\Bigg\{\displaylines{1 - cos(x) \neq 0 \\\ \dfrac{1 + cos(x)}{1 - cos(x)} \geqslant 0}$

$\iff cos(x) \neq 1$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{x \neq 0 \\\ x \neq \pi}$

$\dfrac{1 + cos(x)}{1 - cos(x)} \geqslant 0$

$\Bigg\{\displaylines{1 + cos(x) \geqslant 0 \\\ 1 - cos(x) \geqslant 0}$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{cos(x) \geqslant 0 \\\ cos(x) \le 1}$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{x \in (-\dfrac{\pi}{2}; \dfrac{\pi}{2}) \\\ x \in (\dfrac{\pi}{2}; -\dfrac{\pi}{2})}$

$\iff D = \Bbb{R}~\backslash~\{0; \pi\}$

c)

ĐKCĐ $cos(x - \dfrac{\pi}{3}) \neq 0$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{x - \dfrac{\pi}{3} \neq \dfrac{\pi}{2} \\\ x - \dfrac{\pi}{3} \neq \dfrac{3}{2}\pi}$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{x \neq \dfrac{5}{6}\pi \\\ x \neq \dfrac{11}{6}\pi}$

$\iff D = \Bbb{R}~\backslash~\{\dfrac{5}{6}\pi; \dfrac{11}{6}\pi\}$

d)

ĐKCĐ $sin(2x - \dfrac{\pi}{6}) \neq 0$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{2x - \dfrac{\pi}{6} \neq 0 \\\ 2x - \dfrac{\pi}{6} \neq 2\pi}$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{2x \neq \dfrac{\pi}{6} \\\ 2x \neq \dfrac{7}{3}\pi}$

$\iff \Bigg\{\displaylines{x \neq \dfrac{\pi}{12} \\\ x \neq \dfrac{7}{6}\pi}$

$\iff D = \Bbb{R}~\backslash~\{\dfrac{\pi}{12}; \dfrac{7}{6}\pi\}$
